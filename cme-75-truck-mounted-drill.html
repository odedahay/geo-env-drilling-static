Cme 75 Truck Mounted Drill

Overview

Considered to be the workhorse of the CME line of drills these rigs will be able to complete your drill program no matter what the soil or rock conditions.

CME 75 Tooling

  • 4" and 6" Solid Augers
  • 2.75" - 10.25" ID Hollow Augers
  • NQ, HQ and PQ Diamond Core Barrels
  • 3.25", 5.25" and 6.25" Tricone
  • CME Continuous Soil Sampler (3.5" ID)
  • CME 140 lb. Auto Hammer for SP Testing
