About Our Company

Geo-Environmental Drilling Inc.

Since 1991, Geo-Environmental Drilling Inc. has set the benchmark for excellence in the drilling industry. Our mission is to deliver dependable, professional drilling services efficiently, while upholding a steadfast commitment to safety, training, equipment diversity, environmental responsibility, and unparalleled customer service. We strive to exceed client expectations by ensuring our team is equipped with the latest skills and knowledge to work safely, utilizing a diverse range of equipment, prioritizing environmental stewardship in all operations, and delivering experiences that build lasting relationships fortified by trust and satisfaction. Guided by our core values, we provide innovative solutions while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

THE SUPPORT TEAM

At Geo, we recognize that every project is unique. That’s why we work closely with our clients to ensure seamless execution from planning through completion. Our experienced management team, skilled field crews, dedicated operations staff, and behind the scenes maintenance team work together to keep projects running efficiently and effectively.

When you choose Geo for your next project, you're partnering with a team committed to delivering optimal results while minimizing impact and maximizing value.

HEALTH & SAFETY

At Geo-Environmental Drilling Inc., the health and safety of our employees and clients is paramount. We take proactive measures to foster a culture of safety that emphasizes risk prevention, accountability and prioritizes the well-being of everyone involved in our operations.

Our participation in the WSIB Excellence Program underscores our dedication to maintaining exemplary health and safety practices. Through ongoing education and training, our team is equipped with the knowledge and skills required to navigate the complexities of various drilling projects with confidence. Our strict adherence to industry regulations and best practices ensures that our operations meet and exceed established safety standards.

These efforts are a testament to our unwavering dedication to protecting our workforce and delivering safe, reliable services to our valued clients.
